## Recommendations 📋 (deliberately not done — bigger than this pass)

- **Rate-limit `/api/assessment/submit`**: it is unauthenticated by design
  (public placement flow) and each call invokes Gemini — a cost-abuse vector.
  Options: Vercel WAF rule, per-IP throttle in the route, or a captcha on the
  contact form. Same applies to `/api/assessment/questions`.
- **No test suite**: CI covers types/lint/i18n/build only. Highest-value first
  tests would be `answerUtils.normalizeAnswer` (fraction grading) and
  `mockExamSelection` (group atomicity) — both pure functions, easy to test.
- **`signUpStudent`/`signUpParent` race**: two rapid submits could hit
  Supabase's email-rate limits; the generic error message covers it, but a
  disabled-while-pending submit already mitigates in practice.
- **Legacy `questions`/`question_categories` tables** are still queried by the
  wrong-question bank + variations flow but empty of new content — a future
  consolidation onto `assessment_questions` would remove the dual-schema split
  (see CLAUDE.md「雙表並存」note).
